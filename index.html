Stratus includes built-in privacy features out of the box that prevent data tracking and ensure your browsing history stays private. No one can access your browsing history (apart from you).

Tracker Blocking

We have created a feature that, before visiting the site, Stratus will check for any known trackers, block them, and then the website will load tracker free!

Cache clearing

Stratus will clear your cache every hour. By removing stored data (cache), websites you've visited are no longer stored locally, which reduces the traceability of your web activities.
